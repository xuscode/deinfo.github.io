ABOUT

We focus on providing consulting and implementation of integrated information solutions for enterprises, and are committed to providing advanced and complete BIM/BLM solutions and engineering consulting services to customers in the construction industry to enhance their core competitiveness. The company's aim is to provide each customer with the highest quality, most professional and most satisfactory service. In the field of civil engineering, the company has the BLM/BIM lifecycle implementation capability, providing a full lifecycle solution from design, construction to operation and maintenance management. At the same time, the company has the software secondary development capability based on the mainstream BIM platform, and on this basis formed a series of BIM-based forward design solutions. Since its establishment, the company has been based on honesty and innovation, and has continuously improved its ability to serve customers. So far, it has cooperated with many well-known enterprises at home and abroad, such as: Shanghai Municipal Engineering Design and Research Institute (Group) Co., Ltd., Shanghai Urban Construction Design Research Institute (Group) Co., Ltd., Tongji University Architectural Design and Research Institute ( Group) Co., Ltd., China Design Group Co., Ltd., etc.
